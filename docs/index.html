ImageSquee - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Overview

ImageSquee is an image compression utility for Android. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

ImageSquee does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not require or request internet access.

Image Processing

When you share images to ImageSquee, the app processes them entirely on your device. Images are resized, compressed, and optionally stripped of EXIF metadata (including GPS location, camera information, and timestamps). Processed images are stored temporarily in the app's private cache directory and are automatically cleaned up on subsequent use.

Permissions

ImageSquee does not request any special permissions. It receives images only through the Android Share system, which grants temporary read access to the shared content.

Third-Party Services

ImageSquee does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

ImageSquee does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
