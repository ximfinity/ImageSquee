FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars):
ImageSquee sits quietly in your Share menu, ready to squeeze your photos down to size before they reach their destination.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Select one or more photos in any app
2. Tap Share and choose ImageSquee
3. Your images are instantly resized, compressed, and stripped of metadata
4. A new Share menu appears so you can send the optimized images wherever you like

That's it. No extra steps, no clutter, no account required.

WHY USE IMAGESQUEE?
- Save mobile data by sharing smaller files
- Protect your privacy by stripping GPS coordinates, camera info, timestamps, and other EXIF metadata from every photo
- Send images faster over slow connections
- Avoid file size limits on messaging apps and email

FEATURES
- Supports single or multi-image sharing from any app
- Output to WebP or JPEG format
- Adjustable target resolution: 480p, 720p, 1080p, 1440p, or 4K
- Compression quality slider from 1% to 100%
- Full EXIF/metadata stripping (location, camera model, timestamps, and more)
- Minimal processing overlay — no app switching or waiting screens
- Settings are saved and remembered between uses
- No ads. No tracking. No internet permission. Your photos stay on your device.

PRIVACY
ImageSquee does not request internet access. It cannot upload, transmit, or share your photos with anyone. Processing happens entirely on your device. Metadata stripping removes GPS coordinates, camera make/model, timestamps, artist info, and other identifying data.

PAID, NOT FREE
This is a one-time purchase with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions. You pay once and own it forever.

Requires Android 12 or later.
